About SteadyStride

SteadyStride is a revolutionary anti-tremor band designed to improve the quality of life for individuals suffering from various tremor disorders. Our team of dedicated engineers, neuroscientists, and healthcare professionals has developed a cutting-edge solution that combines advanced sensor technology with machine learning algorithms to provide personalized tremor reduction.

Our mission is to empower those affected by tremors to regain control of their daily activities and live life to the fullest. We are committed to ongoing research and development to continually improve our technology and expand its applications.
